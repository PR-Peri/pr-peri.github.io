About Me

Hey, I’m an AI engineer who loves building smart systems that actually solve problems. I work on turning data and algorithms into real solutions that help people make better decisions and build better products.

My work revolves around machine learning, automation, and model experimentation. I enjoy designing systems that perform well but also make sense in the real world. I focus more on practical impact than chasing perfect metrics.

I’m always exploring new ways to improve workflows, optimize models, and create tools that make AI more useful and accessible. The field keeps changing fast, and I enjoy staying at the edge of that evolution.

This site is where I share some of my projects, experiments, and lessons I’ve learned along the way. If you’re into AI, data, or tech in general, feel free to reach out as I’m always open to exchanging ideas or collaborating on cool things.
